Common Excersises

The following exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpee
Burpees are great, full body excercise to increase your strength and endurance. begin in a standing postiotn, drop into a squat and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and than forward again quickly, and than jump up from a squatted positon
plank
Planks build your core strength. To preform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position for as long as you can
Mountain climbers
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up postion, then bring one knee up to your chest than switch as quickly as you can ( as though you are climbing a mountian).
